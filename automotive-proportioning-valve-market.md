<p><ul><li>Master Cylinder-Mounted Automotive Proportioning Valve</li><li>Load Sensing Automotive Proportioning Valve</li><li>Combination Valve-Mounted Automotive Proportioning Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Proportioning Valve market consists of three main types: Master Cylinder-Mounted Proportioning Valve, Load Sensing Proportioning Valve, and Combination Valve-Mounted Proportioning Valve. The Master Cylinder-Mounted valve adjusts brake pressure based on the input from the master cylinder. The Load Sensing valve adjusts brake pressure depending on the vehicle's load. The Combination Valve-Mounted valve combines the functions of a proportioning valve with other components in the valve system. Each type serves a specific purpose in ensuring optimal brake performance in different driving conditions.</p></p>
