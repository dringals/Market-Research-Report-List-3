<p><p>Flexible filament material is a type of 3D printing material that allows for bending and stretching of printed objects. Rigid filament material is the opposite, providing sturdy and inflexible prints. Semi-flexible filament material falls in between, offering a balance of flexibility and rigidity. Each type caters to different applications and preferences in the 3D printing industry, with flexible filament material being suitable for items like phone cases, rigid filament material for architectural models, and semi-flexible filament material for prototypes requiring a mix of properties.</p></p>
