<p><p>Fork sensors are photoelectric sensors used for precise detection of objects on conveyor belts in industrial automation applications. These sensors have a forked shape with an emitter and receiver located at the opposite ends of the fork, allowing them to detect objects passing between the arms of the fork. Fork sensors are commonly used in packaging, food and beverage, automotive, and pharmaceutical industries for tasks such as counting, sorting, and detecting product presence.</p><p>The Fork Sensors Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.1% during the forecast period. <p><ul><li>PNP-NO</li><li>PNP-NC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fork sensors in the PNP-NO market type operate on a normally open configuration, meaning the sensor output is turned off when an object is detected. Conversely, sensors in the PNP-NC market type operate on a normally closed configuration, where the sensor output is turned on when an object is detected. Both types of sensors are commonly used in industrial automation and manufacturing applications to detect objects passing through the sensor's field of view.</p></p>
