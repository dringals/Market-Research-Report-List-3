<p><p>Carbon fiber sizing agents are used to improve the adhesion between the carbon fiber and the matrix material in composite manufacturing. There are two main types of carbon fiber sizing agents: Dispersion type and Emulsion type. </p><p>The Dispersion type sizing agents are typically in powder form and are mixed with the carbon fibers through dry blending or during the manufacturing process. </p><p>On the other hand, Emulsion type sizing agents are liquid-based and are sprayed or coated onto the carbon fibers. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing the performance of composite materials.</p></p>
