<p><strong>The Dissolved Oxygen Measurement Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polographic</li><li>Galvanic</li><li>Optical(Luminescent)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dissolved Oxygen Measurement Devices are categorized into three main types: Polarographic, Galvanic, and Optical (Luminescent). Polarographic sensors measure dissolved oxygen by monitoring the current generated while Galvanic sensors measure oxygen content through a chemical reaction. Optical or Luminescent sensors use fluorescence quenching to determine oxygen levels. Each type of sensor has its own advantages and limitations, making them suitable for different applications in industries such as wastewater treatment, aquaculture, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
