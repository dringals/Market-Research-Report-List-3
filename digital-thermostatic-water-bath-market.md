<p><p>Digital Thermostatic Water Bath comes in different market types based on the number of holes present for holding samples. Single hole water baths are designed for holding a single sample at a time, providing precise temperature control. Two-hole water baths allow for holding two samples simultaneously, increasing efficiency in testing procedures. Porous market water baths have multiple holes arranged in a grid pattern, enabling users to conduct multiple experiments at once. Each type offers unique advantages depending on the specific needs of the laboratory.</p></p>
