<p><p>Oil-trunk pumps are used in the oil and gas industry to extract oil from underground reservoirs. There are two main types of oil-trunk pumps: tube pumps and rod pumps. Tube pumps use a long, flexible tube to bring oil to the surface, while rod pumps use a series of rods connected to a pump at the bottom of the well. Both types of pumps are essential in the oil and gas industry for efficiently extracting oil from deep underground reservoirs.</p></p>
