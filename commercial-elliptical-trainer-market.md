<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Commercial Elliptical Trainer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Front-drive Ellipticals</li><li>Rear-drive Ellipticals</li><li>Center-drive Ellipticals</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial elliptical trainers come in three main types: front-drive, rear-drive, and center-drive. Front-drive ellipticals have the flywheel placed at the front of the machine, providing a smoother and more natural stride. Rear-drive ellipticals have the flywheel at the back, offering a more compact design and greater stability. Center-drive ellipticals have the flywheel positioned in the middle, giving users a more balanced workout experience. Each type has its own unique benefits, catering to different preferences and needs in the commercial market.</p></p>
