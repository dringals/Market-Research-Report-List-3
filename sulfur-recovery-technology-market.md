<p><strong>The Sulfur Recovery Technology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Claus Process</li><li>Tail Gas Treatment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sulfur Recovery Technology can be classified into two main types: Claus Process and Tail Gas Treatment. The Claus Process is a widely used method for sulfur recovery in refineries and natural gas processing plants, where sulfur compounds are converted into elemental sulfur. On the other hand, Tail Gas Treatment technologies are designed to further reduce sulfur emissions by treating the remaining sulfur compounds in the exhaust gases from the Claus Process. Both technologies play a crucial role in minimizing sulfur emissions and ensuring environmental compliance in industrial processes.</p></p>
