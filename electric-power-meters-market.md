<p><p>Electric Power Meters market is categorized into three main types based on the type of power they measure: Active Power Electric Power Meters, Reactive Power Electric Power Meters, and Apparent Power Electric Power Meters. Active power meters measure the actual power consumption, reactive power meters measure the power that moves back and forth between the source and load, and apparent power meters measure the total power consumed by a system. Each type of power meter plays a crucial role in accurately measuring and monitoring power usage in various settings.</p></p>
