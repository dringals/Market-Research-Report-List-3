<p><p>A soil gas sampler is a device used to collect and analyze gas samples from the subsurface soil. It is commonly used in environmental assessments to detect the presence of volatile organic compounds (VOCs), methane, and other gases that may indicate soil contamination or underground leaks. The sampler consists of a probe that is inserted into the soil to collect gas samples, which are then analyzed in a laboratory for further investigation.</p><p>The Soil Gas Sampler Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.2% during the forecast period.
