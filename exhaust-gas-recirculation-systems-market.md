<p><p>Exhaust Gas Recirculation (EGR) Systems are designed to reduce emissions of nitrogen oxides (NOx) from the exhaust gases of internal combustion engines. The system works by recirculating a portion of the engine's exhaust back into the combustion chamber, which lowers the combustion temperature and reduces the formation of NOx gases. EGR systems are widely utilized in various vehicles, including passenger cars, trucks, and commercial vehicles, to comply with emission regulations and improve fuel efficiency.</p><p>The Exhaust Gas Recirculation Systems Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.6% during the forecast period.
