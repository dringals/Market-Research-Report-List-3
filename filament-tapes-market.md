<p><p>Filament tapes market consists of two main types: Single Sided Tapes and Double Sided Tapes. Single sided tapes have adhesive on only one side and are commonly used for packaging and sealing cartons. Double sided tapes have adhesive on both sides and are often used for mounting and bonding applications. Both types of tapes are reinforced with filaments for added strength and durability, making them ideal for heavy-duty and high-stress applications.</p></p>
