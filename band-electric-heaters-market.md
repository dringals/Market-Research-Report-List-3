<p><p>Ceramic band heaters are designed with a ceramic core that provides efficient and uniform heat distribution. They are commonly used in high-temperature industrial applications due to their durability and ability to withstand extreme temperatures. Mica band heaters, on the other hand, use mica insulation to provide excellent thermal conductivity and insulation. They are often used in applications where precise temperature control is required. Both types of band heaters are popular choices for heating cylindrical objects such as pipes, cylinders, and drums in various industries.</p></p>
