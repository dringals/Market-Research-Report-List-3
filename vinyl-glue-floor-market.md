<p><p>Vinyl glue floor market can be categorized into two types: movable type and fixed type. Movable type refers to vinyl flooring that can be easily removed and reinstalled in a different location, providing flexibility and convenience. On the other hand, fixed type vinyl flooring is permanently adhered to the subfloor, offering durability and stability. Both types cater to different consumer needs and preferences, with movable type being suitable for those who value versatility and ease of installation, while fixed type is preferred for long-term use and reliability.</p></p>
