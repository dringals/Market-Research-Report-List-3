<p><ul><li>Powder Triple Super Phosphate</li><li>Particle Triple Super Phosphate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Triple Super Phosphate (TSP) is available in two main market types: powder TSP and particle TSP. Powder TSP consists of finely ground granules, making it easy to mix with other fertilizers and apply evenly on crops. On the other hand, particle TSP is made up of larger granules, allowing for slower release of phosphorus into the soil. Both types of TSP are widely used in agriculture to provide essential phosphorus nutrients to plants for healthy growth and development.</p></p>
