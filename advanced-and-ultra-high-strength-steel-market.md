<p><p>Advanced high-strength steel (AHSS) refers to steel with a tensile strength ranging from 550 to 780 MPa, offering a good balance of strength and formability for automotive applications. On the other hand, ultra-high-strength steel exceeds 780 MPa in tensile strength, providing exceptional strength and durability for demanding applications such as aerospace and defense. Both types of steel are high-performance materials that offer lightweighting potential and improved crash performance, making them increasingly popular in various industries.</p></p>
