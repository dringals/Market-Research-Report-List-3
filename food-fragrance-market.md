<p><p>Food fragrance market can be classified into two types: natural extract and synthetic. Natural extract fragrances are derived from plants, fruits, and herbs through processes like steam distillation and cold pressing. These fragrances are sought after for their purity and authenticity. On the other hand, synthetic fragrances are created in laboratories using chemical compounds to replicate natural scents. They are often preferred for their consistency and cost-effectiveness. Both types of fragrances play a crucial role in enhancing the taste and aroma of various food products.</p></p>
