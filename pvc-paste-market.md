<p><ul><li>Micro Suspension Method</li><li>Emulsion Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PVC paste market consists of two main types: Micro Suspension Method and Emulsion Method. The Micro Suspension Method involves the blending of PVC resins in a solvent to create a paste-like consistency, while the Emulsion Method involves the polymerization of vinyl chloride in water to form PVC paste. Both methods have their own advantages and applications in various industries such as construction, automotive, and packaging. The choice between the two methods depends on the specific requirements of the end use and production process.</p></p>
