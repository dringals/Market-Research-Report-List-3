<p><p>A self-priming pump is a type of centrifugal pump that is designed to eliminate the need for external priming mechanisms. This type of pump can automatically prime itself and operate effectively even when there is air or gas present in the fluid being pumped. Self-priming pumps are commonly used in applications where there is a likelihood of air or gas being present in the fluid, such as in wastewater treatment plants, industrial processes, and irrigation systems.</p><p>The self-priming pump market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.1% during the forecast period.
