<p><p>Children electric toothbrush market includes two main types - Vibration and Rotation-Oscillation. Vibration toothbrushes use vibrating bristles to clean teeth, while Rotation-Oscillation toothbrushes have circular rotating bristles that oscillate back and forth to remove plaque and debris effectively. Vibration toothbrushes are usually gentler and less abrasive, making them suitable for children with sensitive gums. On the other hand, Rotation-Oscillation toothbrushes are known for their thorough cleaning action, making them a popular choice for children who need more intense plaque removal.</p></p>
