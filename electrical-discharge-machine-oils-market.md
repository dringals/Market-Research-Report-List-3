<p><p>Electrical Discharge Machine (EDM) oils are divided into two main types: natural-based and synthetic-based. Natural-based EDM oils are derived from mineral or plant sources and are known for their high lubricity and cooling properties. On the other hand, synthetic-based EDM oils are manufactured using chemical processes and offer superior thermal stability and oxidation resistance. Both types are widely used in the manufacturing industry for EDM operations to enhance the cutting performance and extend the tool life.</p></p>
