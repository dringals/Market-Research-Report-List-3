Lecithin, derived from soybeans, is a natural emulsifier used in a variety of food products. Sorbitan esters and stearoyl lactylates are also commonly used as emulsifiers in food products. Polyglycerol esters are derived from plant-based sources and are used as emulsifiers in various food applications.</p></p>
