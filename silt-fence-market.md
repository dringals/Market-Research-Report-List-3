<p><p>Silt fences are commonly categorized into two types: woven fibers and non-woven fibers. Woven fiber silt fences are made from tightly woven fabric materials, providing a strong barrier against sediment runoff. On the other hand, non-woven fiber silt fences are made from bonded fibers that are entangled together, offering effective filtration and erosion control. Both types of silt fences are essential in managing sediment and protecting the environment from pollution caused by construction activities.</p></p>
