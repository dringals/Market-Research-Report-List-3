<p><p>Electrostatic separator market is categorized into two main types: dry and wet separators. On the other hand, wet electrostatic separators are designed to separate wet materials based on their different electrostatic properties in a liquid medium. Both types of separators have their unique advantages and are commonly used in industries such as mining, recycling, and environmental remediation.</p></p>
