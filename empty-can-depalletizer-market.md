<p><p>Empty can depalletizers are divided into two main types based on their design and operation: high-level depalletizers and low-level depalletizers. High-level depalletizers are designed to handle pallets at a higher level, reducing the need for manual labor during the depalletizing process. On the other hand, low-level depalletizers are positioned closer to the ground, making them more suitable for facilities with space constraints. Both types offer efficient solutions for automated depalletizing of empty cans in the packaging industry.</p></p>
