<p><p>Functional silanes are categorized into different types based on their chemical composition and properties. These include Sulfur Silanes, which contain sulfur atoms and are used for improving adhesion and cross-linking in rubber compounds. Vinyl Silanes are utilized as coupling agents in polyolefins and elastomers. Amino Silanes are employed as adhesion promoters and surface modifiers. Epoxy Silanes are used for enhancing adhesion and chemical resistance in coatings. Alkyl Silanes act as water repellents and adhesion promoters. Methacryloxy Silanes are used in thermosetting resins and coatings. Other types of functional silanes include fluorosilanes, mercaptosilanes, and silane coupling agents.</p></p>
