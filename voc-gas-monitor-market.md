<p><ul><li>PID</li><li>Metal-oxide Semiconductor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>VOC Gas Monitor market types include PID (Photoionization Detector) and Metal-oxide Semiconductor (MOS) monitors. PID monitors use ultraviolet light to ionize volatile organic compounds, making them easily detectable. MOS monitors rely on a semiconducting metal oxide to detect VOCs by changes in electrical conductivity. While PID monitors are more sensitive and selective, MOS monitors are cost-effective and versatile for various applications. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring safety and preventing exposure to harmful VOCs in various industries.</p></p>
