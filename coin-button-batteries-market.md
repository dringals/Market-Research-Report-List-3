<p><ul><li>LR (Alkaline)</li><li>SR (Silver Oxide)</li><li>CR (Lithium)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Coin button batteries are categorized into LR (alkaline), SR (silver oxide), CR (lithium), and other types based on the materials used in their construction. LR batteries are typically used in low-drain devices, SR batteries are commonly found in watches and calculators due to their high energy density, and CR batteries are used in high-drain devices like cameras and medical devices. Other types of coin button batteries may include zinc-air or nickel-metal hydride batteries, each suited for specific applications.</p></p>
