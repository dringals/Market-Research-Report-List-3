<p><p>A Static Synchronous Compensator (STATCOM) is a power-electronics-based device used to provide reactive power support and voltage regulation in power systems. There are two main types of STATCOMs based on voltage levels: Low Voltage STATCOM and High Voltage STATCOM. Low Voltage STATCOMs are typically used in distribution systems to improve voltage stability and reduce losses. High Voltage STATCOMs, on the other hand, are used in transmission systems to enhance grid stability and control power flow. Both types play a critical role in optimizing power system operation.</p></p>
