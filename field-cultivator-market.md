<p><strong>The Field Cultivator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Folding Field Cultivator</li><li>Fixed Field Cultivator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Field cultivators are commonly categorized into two main types: folding field cultivators and fixed field cultivators. Folding field cultivators are designed to be easily transported from one field to another as they can be folded up for compact storage. On the other hand, fixed field cultivators are more suitable for larger fields where constant cultivation is required. Both types of cultivators serve the same purpose of preparing the soil for planting by breaking up clods and creating a smooth seedbed.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Field Cultivator Market Industry Research by Application is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mounted</li><li>Trailed</li><li>Semi-mounted</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Field cultivators are agricultural machinery used for soil preparation before planting. They come in three main variations: mounted, trailed, and semi-mounted. Mounted cultivators are attached to the tractor, offering easy maneuverability in small fields. Trailed cultivators are towed behind the tractor, suitable for larger fields with straight rows. Semi-mounted cultivators combine the features of both mounted and trailed cultivators, providing a compromise between maneuverability and efficiency. Each type caters to different farm sizes and terrain, ensuring optimal soil preparation for planting.</p></p>
