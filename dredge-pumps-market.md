<p><p>Dredge pumps are used in various applications such as dredging, mining, and wastewater treatment. There are three main types of dredge pumps: horizontal dredge pumps, vertical dredge pumps, and submersible dredge pumps. Horizontal dredge pumps are typically used for high flow, low head applications, while vertical dredge pumps are suitable for applications where space is limited. Submersible dredge pumps are designed to be fully submerged in the fluid being pumped, making them ideal for underwater dredging operations.</p></p>
