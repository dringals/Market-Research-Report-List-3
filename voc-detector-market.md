<p><strong>The VOC Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PID</li><li>Metal-oxide Semiconductor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>VOC detectors are used to monitor volatile organic compounds in various industries. Two common types are PID and Metal-oxide Semiconductor detectors. PID detectors use a UV lamp to ionize compounds, providing real-time detection with high accuracy. Metal-oxide Semiconductor detectors rely on a chemical reaction between VOCs and a metal oxide, offering continuous monitoring capabilities. Each type has its advantages and is suitable for different applications based on factors like sensitivity, range, and response time. Both play a crucial role in ensuring safe and healthy indoor air quality.</p></p>
