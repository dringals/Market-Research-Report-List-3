<p><p>Shelf life testing is necessary to determine the effectiveness and durability of products over time. Real-time shelf life testing involves monitoring products for an extended period under normal storage conditions to assess their stability. Accelerated shelf life testing, on the other hand, speeds up the process by subjecting products to harsher conditions such as higher temperatures and humidity levels to predict their shelf life in a shorter time frame. Both methods help manufacturers ensure the quality and safety of their products.</p></p>
