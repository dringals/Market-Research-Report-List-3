<p><p>Paper machine oils can be categorized into two main types: synthetic oils and mineral oils. Synthetic oils are man-made lubricants that are specifically designed for high-performance applications in paper machines. They offer superior lubrication properties and thermal stability compared to mineral oils. Mineral oils, on the other hand, are derived from natural sources such as petroleum. They are generally more cost-effective but may not provide the same level of performance as synthetic oils in demanding paper machine operations.</p></p>
