Mineral fiber ceiling tiles are made from natural or synthetic minerals and provide good sound absorption and fire resistance. Metal ceiling tiles are durable and versatile, often used in commercial spaces. Gypsum ceiling tiles are lightweight and easy to install, offering good insulation properties. Other types of ceiling tiles may include fiberglass, PVC, or wood, each with its unique properties and applications in various industries.</p></p>
