<p><strong>The Hydraulic Tappet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plunger</li><li>Check Valve</li><li>One-way Valve Spring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hydraulic tappet market offers three main types: Plunger, Check Valve, and One-way Valve Spring. Plunger tappets operate by using a hydraulic piston to adjust valve clearance. Check Valve tappets utilize a check valve to regulate oil flow and maintain proper operation. One-way Valve Spring tappets incorporate a one-way valve spring mechanism to control the flow of oil to adjust valve clearance. Each type offers unique advantages and applications, catering to different preferences and requirements in the automotive industry.</p></p>
