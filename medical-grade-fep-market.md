<p><p>Medical Grade FEP is classified into two main types - Extrusion Grade and Injection Grade. Extrusion Grade FEP is used for creating tubing, films, and wires in medical devices, due to its excellent flexibility and chemical resistance. Injection Grade FEP, on the other hand, is used for manufacturing intricate parts with high precision and clarity, such as surgical instruments and components. Both types of FEP play a crucial role in the medical industry by providing durable and safe materials for various applications.</p></p>
