<p><p>Anti-sniper Detection System for Defense market includes three main types - Infrared, Laser, and Acoustic. Infrared systems use thermal imaging to detect the heat signature of a sniper's weapon or body. Laser systems use laser beams to detect the presence of a sniper by measuring disruptions in the beam caused by objects in its path. Acoustic systems use microphones to detect the sound of a sniper's shot and pinpoint its location. Each type has its own strengths and limitations, catering to different defense needs.</p></p>
