<p><ul><li>Light Brown Sugar</li><li>Dark Brown Sugar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Natural brown sugar is available in two main market types: light brown sugar and dark brown sugar. Light brown sugar is a lighter shade of brown due to a lower molasses content, while dark brown sugar has a higher molasses content, resulting in a darker color and richer flavor. Light brown sugar is commonly used in baking, while dark brown sugar is preferred for recipes that require a more intense molasses flavor. Both types of brown sugar are natural sweeteners and can be used interchangeably in most recipes.</p></p>
