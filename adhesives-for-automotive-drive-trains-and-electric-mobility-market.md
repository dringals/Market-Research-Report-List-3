<p><p>Adhesives used in automotive drive trains and electric mobility can be classified into three main types: liquid, solid, and paste. Liquid adhesives are flowable and can be easily applied onto surfaces before hardening. Solid adhesives come in the form of preformed shapes that are heated and melted to create a bond. Paste adhesives have a thick consistency and are often used for filling gaps and bonding irregular surfaces. Each type of adhesive offers unique benefits and applications in the automotive industry.</p></p>
