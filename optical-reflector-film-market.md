<p><ul><li>Non-metallic Films</li><li>Metallic Films</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical reflector film comes in two main types - non-metallic films and metallic films. Non-metallic films are typically made of materials like dielectric coatings or multilayered plastics that reflect light without the use of metals. They are often used in applications where lightweight and flexibility are important. Metallic films, on the other hand, incorporate metals like aluminum to achieve high reflectivity and durability. They are commonly used in electronic displays, automobile headlights, and solar panels where a strong reflective surface is required.</p></p>
