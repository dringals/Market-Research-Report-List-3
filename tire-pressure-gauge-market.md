<p><p>Tire pressure gauges are available in two main types: dial type and digital type. Dial type gauges feature a traditional analog dial face with a needle that indicates the pressure reading. They are simple to use and rely on mechanical components for accuracy. Digital type gauges, on the other hand, have a digital screen that displays the exact pressure measurement. They are often more precise and easier to read than dial gauges, making them popular among car enthusiasts and professionals. Both types serve the same purpose of ensuring proper tire inflation for optimal performance and safety.</p></p>
