<p><p>In-Display Fingerprint Sensor market consists of three main types: Optical, Ultrasonic, and Capacitive. Optical In-Display Fingerprint Sensors use light to scan fingerprints, Ultrasonic Sensors use sound waves for more accurate readings, and Capacitive Sensors rely on electrical currents to detect fingerprint patterns. Each type has its own strengths and weaknesses, with Ultrasonic Sensors being more secure and accurate, while Optical Sensors are more affordable and widely used. The market for these sensors continues to grow as demand for convenient and secure biometric technology increases.</p></p>
