<p><p>Silicon Anti-Reflective Coatings (SiARCs) are used to reduce reflection on surfaces, such as glass or solar panels. Single layer SiARCs provide a basic level of protection against glare and reflection, while double layer SiARCs offer enhanced performance by minimizing light reflection even further.
