<p><p>Nuclear power plant market consists of different types of reactors such as Pressurized Water Reactor, Boiling Water Reactor, Heavy Water Reactor, and Fast Reactor. Pressurized Water Reactors use pressurized water to transfer heat from the reactor core to generate electricity. Boiling Water Reactors have the water in the core boil and turn into steam to power turbines. Heavy Water Reactors use heavy water as a moderator and coolant. Fast Reactors use fast neutrons to sustain the nuclear chain reaction.</p></p>
