<p><p>Continuous reactors are used in various industries for continuous production processes. The main types of continuous reactors include tubular reactors, fixed bed reactors, fluid bed reactors, and continuous stirred tank reactors. Tubular reactors consist of a long tube where reactions take place. Fixed bed reactors have a stationary bed of catalyst where reactions occur. Fluid bed reactors use a bed of solid particles suspended in a fluid for reactions. Continuous stirred tank reactors have a well-mixed tank for continuous reaction processes. Each type of reactor has its advantages and applications in different industries.</p></p>
