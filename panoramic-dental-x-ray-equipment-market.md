<p><p>Panoramic dental X-ray equipment comes in two main types: digital and analog. Digital panoramic X-ray machines use digital sensors to capture images, which can then be viewed and stored electronically. This type of equipment offers faster processing times and easier image manipulation. On the other hand, analog panoramic X-ray machines use traditional film to capture images, which must be developed before viewing. Digital equipment is gradually replacing analog equipment due to its many advantages, though analog machines are still used in some dental practices.</p></p>
