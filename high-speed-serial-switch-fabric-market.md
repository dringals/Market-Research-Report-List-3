Network core switching focuses on high-speed data transfer within the core network. VLAN switching separates traffic into different virtual LANs for better network management. Other types of switch fabrics may include specialized functionalities such as security features or integration with specific network protocols. These different types cater to varying network requirements and applications.</p></p>
