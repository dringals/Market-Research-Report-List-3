<p><p>Pure Iron Powder Market Types include Carbonyl Iron Powder and Electrolytic Iron Powder. Carbonyl Iron Powder is produced by the thermal decomposition of iron pentacarbonyl, resulting in high purity and spherical particles. It is used in a wide range of applications such as diamond tool production and magnetic materials. On the other hand, Electrolytic Iron Powder is produced through an electrolysis process, resulting in irregular shaped particles with high purity. It is commonly used in the manufacturing of batteries and welding electrodes.</p></p>
