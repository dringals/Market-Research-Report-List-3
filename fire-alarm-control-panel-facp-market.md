<p><ul><li>Conventional</li><li>Addressable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fire Alarm Control Panel (FACP) Market types include Conventional and Addressable systems. Conventional FACP use zones to detect the location of a fire, providing a general area of alarm activation. Addressable FACP pinpoint the exact location of a fire by assigning a unique address to each device on the system. Conventional systems are generally more cost-effective and simpler to install, while Addressable systems offer more precise information and easier troubleshooting. Both types play a crucial role in fire detection and safety.</p></p>
