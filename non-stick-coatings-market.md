<p><p>Non-stick coatings market can be categorized into three main types: PTFE, PFA, and FEP. PTFE coatings, also known as Teflon, are popular for their high heat resistance and durability. PFA coatings offer excellent chemical resistance and non-stick properties. FEP coatings are valued for their low friction and non-reactivity with chemicals. Each type of coating has its own unique set of benefits and applications, catering to different industries and consumer needs in the non-stick coatings market.</p></p>
