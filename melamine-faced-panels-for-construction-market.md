<p><ul><li>MF-C</li><li>MF-MDF</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Melamine-faced panels for construction are popularly used in two main types of markets: MF-C (Melamine-faced Chipboard) and MF-MDF (Melamine-faced Medium Density Fiberboard). MF-C panels are made from small wood chips bonded together with resin and then covered with a melamine laminate for a durable finish. MF-MDF panels are similar, but made from a denser fiberboard material for increased strength and stability. Both types of panels are widely used in furniture, cabinetry, and interior construction for their affordability, versatility, and easy maintenance.</p></p>
