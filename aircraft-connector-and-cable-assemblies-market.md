<p><p>Aircraft connector and cable assemblies market includes two main types - connectors and cable assemblies. Connectors are devices that establish an electrical connection between different sections of an aircraft's electrical systems, enabling the transfer of power or signals. Cable assemblies are bundles of wires and cables that are securely connected to connectors to transmit power, data, or signals within an aircraft. Both connectors and cable assemblies play a crucial role in ensuring the proper functioning of an aircraft's electrical systems.</p></p>
