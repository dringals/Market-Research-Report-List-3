<p><p>Pedal Electric Cycles come in two main types: Pedal-assisted and Throttle-assisted. In the Pedal-assisted type, the rider must pedal in order to activate the electric motor, providing assistance to make pedaling easier. On the other hand, in Throttle-assisted type, the rider can simply twist a throttle to engage the motor without the need for pedaling. Both types offer different riding experiences and cater to varying preferences and needs of riders, making them popular choices in the electric cycle market.</p></p>
