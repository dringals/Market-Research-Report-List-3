<p><p>Transfer carts are used in various industries for material handling and transportation purposes. The market for transfer carts can be categorized into three main types: rail transfer carts, trackless transfer carts, and trailer transfer carts. Rail transfer carts run on tracks and are suitable for indoor applications with a fixed path. Trackless transfer carts operate without tracks, providing more flexibility for movement in different directions. Trailer transfer carts are towed by a vehicle and are commonly used for outdoor transportation of heavy loads. Each type offers specific advantages depending on the application requirements.</p></p>
