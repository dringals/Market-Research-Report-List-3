<p><p>The electronic load market consists of three main types: Benchtop, System, and Module. Benchtop electronic loads are standalone devices primarily used for testing and characterizing power supplies and batteries in research and development labs. System electronic loads are part of a larger test system and are typically used for automated testing in manufacturing environments. Module electronic loads are compact units that can be integrated into a larger test setup or system. Each type serves a unique purpose in the electronic load market to meet different testing requirements.</p></p>
