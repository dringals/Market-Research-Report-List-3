<p><p>EMI shielding tapes are used to protect electronic devices from electromagnetic interference. There are two main types of EMI shielding tapes: metal foil and conductive cloth. Metal foil tapes are made of thin sheets of metal like aluminum or copper, which provide excellent shielding properties. Conductive cloth tapes, on the other hand, are woven with conductive fibers to create a barrier against EMI. Both types of tapes are used in various industries to ensure the proper functioning of sensitive electronic equipment.</p></p>
