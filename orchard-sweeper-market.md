<p><p>Orchard sweepers are commonly available in two market types: Self-Propelled Orchard Sweepers and Mounted Orchard Sweepers. Self-Propelled Orchard Sweepers are standalone machines equipped with a motor for propulsion, allowing them to move independently through orchards to collect debris. On the other hand, Mounted Orchard Sweepers are designed to be attached to tractors or other vehicles for transportation and operation. Both types of sweepers serve the same purpose of efficiently cleaning orchards and groves, but they differ in terms of mobility and operation.</p></p>
