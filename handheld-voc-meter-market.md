<p><p>Handheld VOC meters are commonly used for monitoring volatile organic compounds in various industries. There are two main types of sensors used in these devices - PID (Photoionization Detector) and Metal-oxide Semiconductor sensors. PID sensors are effective at detecting a wide range of VOCs, while Metal-oxide Semiconductor sensors are more cost-effective and offer lower detection limits for certain compounds. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in ensuring workplace safety and environmental compliance by quickly and accurately measuring VOC levels in the air.</p></p>
