<p><ul><li>Contact</li><li>Non-Contact</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wall mount foaming soap dispensers are available in two main types- contact and non-contact. Contact dispensers require the user to physically press a button or lever to dispense soap, making direct contact with the dispenser. On the other hand, non-contact dispensers use sensor technology to dispense soap without the user having to touch the dispenser, promoting better hygiene by reducing the spread of germs. Both types cater to different preferences and needs in the market for wall-mounted soap dispensers.</p></p>
