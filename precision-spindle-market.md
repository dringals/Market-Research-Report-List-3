<p><ul><li>Rolling Spindles</li><li>Air Bearing Spindles</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Precision spindles can be categorized into different types based on their design and operation. Rolling spindles use rolling elements like balls or rollers for smooth rotation, making them ideal for high-speed applications. Air bearing spindles use a thin film of air to support the rotating shaft, minimizing friction and wear. Other types of precision spindles include hydrostatic spindles, belt-driven spindles, and motorized spindles. Each type offers specific advantages and is chosen based on the requirements of the application.</p></p>
