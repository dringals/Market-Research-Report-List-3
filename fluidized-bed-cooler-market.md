<p><p>Fluidized Bed Coolers are commonly categorized into two types: Vibrating Fluidized Bed Coolers and Static Fluidized Bed Coolers. Vibrating Fluidized Bed Coolers use vibration to enhance the fluidization process, creating a more efficient cooling system. On the other hand, Static Fluidized Bed Coolers rely on a stationary bed to cool materials. Both types of coolers are used in industries such as agriculture, pharmaceuticals, and food processing to efficiently cool materials in a controlled environment.</p></p>
