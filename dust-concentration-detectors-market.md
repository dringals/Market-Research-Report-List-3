<p><ul><li>Capacitance Measurement</li><li>Beta Ray Measurement</li><li>Light Absorption Measurement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dust concentration detectors are devices used to measure the amount of dust or particles in the air. There are three main types of dust concentration detectors in the market: capacitance measurement, beta ray measurement, and light absorption measurement. Capacitance measurement detectors work by measuring the change in capacitance caused by dust particles. Beta ray measurement detectors use beta radiation to detect dust particles. Light absorption measurement detectors measure the amount of light absorbed by dust particles to determine their concentration in the air.</p></p>
