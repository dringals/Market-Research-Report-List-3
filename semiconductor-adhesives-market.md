<p><ul><li>Liquid</li><li>Solid</li><li>Paste</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Semiconductor Adhesives Market can be classified into three types based on their physical state: Liquid, Solid, and Paste. Liquid adhesives are easy to apply and are commonly used for bonding components in the semiconductor industry. Solid adhesives are preformed shapes that require heat or pressure for bonding. Paste adhesives are a blend of liquid and solid components, offering a balance between easy application and strong bonding properties. Each type of adhesive offers unique advantages and is used for specific applications within the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
