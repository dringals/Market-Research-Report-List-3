<p><p>Automotive gas springs can be categorized into four types: adjustable gas springs, lockable gas springs, traction gas springs, and others. Adjustable gas springs allow for varying levels of force and extension, providing flexibility in adjusting the height and positioning of components. Lockable gas springs can be fixed in a desired position, maintaining stability and security. Traction gas springs provide controlled movement in one direction, ideal for applications requiring smooth and steady operation. Other types of gas springs may include specialty designs tailored to specific automotive needs.</p></p>
