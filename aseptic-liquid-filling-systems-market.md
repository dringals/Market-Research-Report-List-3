<p><p>Aseptic liquid filling systems are categorized into two main types: fully-automatic and semi-automatic. These systems are suitable for large-scale production and can handle a wide range of liquid products. Both types of systems play a vital role in ensuring the aseptic packaging of liquid products.</p></p>
