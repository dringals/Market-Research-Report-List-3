<p><p>Boiler Feed Pumps (BFP) market consists of two main types: Vertical Boiler Feed Pump and Horizontal Boiler Feed Pump. Vertical Boiler Feed Pumps are installed vertically with the motor at the top, while Horizontal Boiler Feed Pumps are mounted horizontally with the motor aligned with the pump shaft. Both types are essential for providing the necessary pressure to feed water into boilers in various industrial applications. The choice between vertical and horizontal pumps depends on factors like space availability and efficiency requirements.</p></p>
