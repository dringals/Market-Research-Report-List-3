MAP is a widely used fertilizer containing high amounts of phosphorus and nitrogen. DAP is another popular choice with balanced nutrients for crops. Rock Phosphate is a natural source of phosphorus that requires processing before use. Super Phosphate is a water-soluble fertilizer known for its quick-release phosphorus content. These different types cater to various agricultural needs and soil conditions.</p></p>
