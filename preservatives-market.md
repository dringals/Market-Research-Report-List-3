<p><p>Preservatives market types include organic solvent preservatives, which are chemical compounds dissolved in organic solvents to prevent microbial growth. Inorganic preservatives are metallic compounds that inhibit microbial growth. Natural antiseptic preservatives are derived from natural sources and have antimicrobial properties. Ester-type preservatives are esters of organic acids that prevent spoilage. Each type of preservative serves a specific purpose in extending the shelf life of products by inhibiting the growth of bacteria, mold, and yeast.</p></p>
