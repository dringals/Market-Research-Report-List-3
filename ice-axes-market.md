<p><p>Ice axes are essential tools for climbers and mountaineers, available in two main market types: bent and straight. Bent ice axes have a curved shaft, allowing for a more ergonomic and natural hand position while ascending steep ice or mixed terrain. They are designed for technical ice climbing and provide better clearance for swings. On the other hand, straight ice axes have a traditional straight shaft, which is ideal for general mountaineering and easier snow climbing. Both types offer different advantages based on the user's specific needs and climbing style.</p></p>
