<p><ul><li>V-FFS</li><li>H-FFS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic case packing machines come in two main types: V-FFS (Vertical Form Fill Seal) and H-FFS (Horizontal Form Fill Seal) machines. V-FFS machines are designed to form, fill, and seal vertical bags, making them suitable for products like snacks, nuts, and coffee beans. On the other hand, H-FFS machines are used for forming, filling, and sealing horizontal bags, making them ideal for products like sandwiches, biscuits, and granola bars. Both types of machines offer efficiency and automation in the case packing process, catering to different packaging needs.</p></p>
