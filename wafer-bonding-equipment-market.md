<p><p>Wafer bonding equipment market can be divided into two main types - fully automatic and semi-automatic. Fully automatic equipment requires minimal human intervention and is capable of performing the bonding process independently. This type of equipment is efficient in high-volume production environments. On the other hand, semi-automatic equipment involves some level of human input and intervention during the bonding process. This type of equipment is often preferred for smaller scale production or when more control over the process is desired.</p></p>
