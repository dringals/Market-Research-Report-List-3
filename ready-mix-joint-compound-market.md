<p><p>Ready Mix Joint Compounds can be classified into two main types: Light Weight Ready Mix Joint Compound and Conventional Ready Mix Joint Compound. </p><p>Light Weight Ready Mix Joint Compound is formulated with lightweight materials such as perlite or vermiculite, making it easier to handle and apply. It is ideal for reducing strain on workers and achieving a smoother finish.</p><p>Conventional Ready Mix Joint Compound, on the other hand, is made with standard materials like gypsum and water. It is versatile and widely used in various applications due to its durability and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
