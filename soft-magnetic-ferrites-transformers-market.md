<p><p>Soft Magnetic Ferrites Transformers are commonly categorized into two main types: Mn-Zn Ferrite Core Transformers and Nickel-Zinc Ferrite Core Transformers. Mn-Zn Ferrites are widely used due to their high magnetic permeability and low losses, making them ideal for applications in power transformers and inductive components. On the other hand, Nickel-Zinc Ferrites offer higher saturation magnetization and are often used in high-frequency applications. Additionally, there are other types of soft magnetic ferrites used in specialized applications, such as lithium ferrites and spinel ferrites.</p></p>
