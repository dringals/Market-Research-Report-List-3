<p><p>Voltage Controlled Temperature Compensated Crystal Oscillator (VCTCXO) is available in three main types according to their output: Output PECL, Output CMOS, and Output SINEWAVE. </p><p>Output PECL VCTCXO provides a PECL (Positive Emitter-Coupled Logic) output signal, suitable for high-speed communication applications. Output CMOS VCTCXO offers a CMOS output signal, which is commonly used in digital circuits for its low power consumption. Output SINEWAVE VCTCXO produces a pure sine wave output signal, ideal for applications where a clean and stable signal is required, such as in RF communications and precision instrumentation.</p></p>
