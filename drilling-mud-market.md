These fluids are seen as more sustainable alternatives to traditional oil-based muds, as they have lower environmental impact and are easier to handle and dispose of. <p><p>Drilling mud market is categorized into different types based on the composition of the fluid used during drilling operations. Oil-Based Fluids (OBF) consist of oil as the continuous phase, providing better lubrication and thermal stability. Water Based Fluids (WBF) are environmentally friendly and cost-effective, utilizing water as the base. Other types include Synthetic-Based Fluids (SBF) and Aerated Fluids, offering unique properties for specific drilling conditions. Each type has its advantages and is chosen based on the requirements of the drilling operation.</p></p>
