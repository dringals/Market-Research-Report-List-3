<p><p>Parking aid sensors are essential safety features in vehicles that help drivers park more easily and avoid collisions. Front parking sensors are positioned on the front bumper to detect objects in front of the vehicle, while rear parking sensors are located on the rear bumper to detect obstacles behind the vehicle. Both types of sensors use ultrasonic technology to measure the distance between the vehicle and objects, providing audible or visual alerts to the driver to prevent accidents while parking.</p></p>
