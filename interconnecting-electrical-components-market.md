<p><p>Interconnecting electrical components market consists of various types including printed circuit boards (PCBs), connectors/sockets, switches, relays, and others. PCBs are used to mechanically support and electrically connect electronic components. Connectors and sockets provide a secure connection between different devices or components. Switches are used to open or close an electrical circuit. Relays are used to control high-voltage circuits with a low-voltage signal. Other components in this market may include wires, cables, and terminals for interconnecting various electrical devices.</p></p>
