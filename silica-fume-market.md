<p><p>Silica fume is classified into three types based on density: densified silica fume, semi densified silica fume, and undensified silica fume. Densified silica fume has the highest density and is typically used in applications where high strength and durability are required. Semi densified silica fume is less dense and has a wider range of applications, while undensified silica fume has the lowest density and is commonly used in applications where flowability is important. These different types cater to various construction needs and requirements.</p></p>
