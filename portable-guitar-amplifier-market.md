<p><p>Portable guitar amplifiers come in two main types: combo amplifiers and head amplifiers. Combo amplifiers include both the amplifier and speaker in one unit, making them easy to transport and set up for gigs or practice sessions. Head amplifiers, on the other hand, separate the amplifier and speaker components, providing more flexibility in terms of customizing the sound. Both types offer compact and lightweight options for guitarists who need to amplify their sound on the go.</p></p>
