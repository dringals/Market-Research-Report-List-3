<p><p>Acoustic Guitar Bridge Market consists mainly of two types - Pin Bridge and Pinless Bridges. Pin Bridge is a traditional design where the strings are held in place by bridge pins. This design allows for easy string changing and better stability. On the other hand, Pinless Bridges have a unique design where the strings are fed through the bridge itself, eliminating the need for bridge pins. This design is known for its ease in string changing and maintaining better string tension and resonance.</p></p>
