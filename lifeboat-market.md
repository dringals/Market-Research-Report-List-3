<p><p>Lifeboat market can be segmented into three main types based on their design and structure: partially enclosed life boats, totally enclosed life boats, and open lifeboats. Partially enclosed life boats provide some protection from the elements but are not fully enclosed like totally enclosed life boats. Totally enclosed life boats offer complete protection and are usually equipped with additional safety features. Open lifeboats are the most basic type, providing minimal protection and are typically used for short distances in calmer waters. Each type caters to different safety requirements and risk factors in marine environments.</p></p>
