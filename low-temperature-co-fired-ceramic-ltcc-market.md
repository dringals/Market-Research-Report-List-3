<p><p>Low Temperature Co-fired Ceramic (LTCC) market includes three main types: LTCC Components, LTCC Substrates, and LTCC Module market. LTCC Components refer to individual parts such as resistors, capacitors, and inductors fabricated using LTCC technology. LTCC Substrates are the base material on which electronic components are mounted and interconnected. LTCC Modules are fully integrated electronic systems incorporating multiple components on a single LTCC substrate. These different types cater to diverse applications in industries like telecommunications, automotive, and aerospace.</p></p>
