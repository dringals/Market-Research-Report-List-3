<p><p>Concrete waterproofing primer is available in two main types: one-component and two-component. One-component primers come ready to use, requiring no mixing or additional components. They are convenient and easy to apply, making them popular for smaller projects. Two-component primers consist of two separate components that need to be mixed together before application. This allows for a more customized and potentially stronger waterproofing solution, making them ideal for larger or more demanding applications. Both types offer effective waterproofing solutions for concrete surfaces.</p></p>
