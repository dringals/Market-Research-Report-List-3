<p><strong>The Hot Melt Granule Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>EVA-base</li><li>PUR-base</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hot melt granules are categorized into two main types: EVA-base and PUR-base. EVA-base hot melt granules are made from ethylene-vinyl acetate copolymers, offering good adhesion and flexibility. They are commonly used in packaging, woodworking, and bookbinding industries. On the other hand, PUR-base hot melt granules are made from polyurethane resins, providing superior adhesion and heat resistance. They are often used in automotive, construction, and electronics applications. Both types of hot melt granules offer efficient bonding solutions for a variety of industries.</p></p>
