<p><p>Lithography overcoats are protective layers applied on top of photoresists in the semiconductor manufacturing process. There are two main types of overcoats: Topcoats and Overcoats. Topcoats are thin layers that improve the resolution and adhesion of photoresists, while Overcoats provide additional protection against moisture, chemicals, and physical damage. Both types of overcoats play a critical role in enhancing the performance and longevity of semiconductor devices by ensuring the quality and reliability of the lithographic process.</p></p>
