<p><p>There are two main types of liquid cold water meters in the market: vertical and level. Vertical liquid cold water meters are designed to be installed vertically with the pipe running horizontally below them. They are commonly used in industrial settings where space is limited. On the other hand, level liquid cold water meters are installed horizontally with the pipe running vertically below them. These meters are frequently used in residential and commercial applications where easy access for reading and maintenance is important.</p></p>
