<p><p>Biobased biodegradable plastics market includes various types such as polyester, PLA, PHA, and cellulose-based plastics. Polyester biobased biodegradable plastics are derived from plant sources and have similar properties to traditional petroleum-based plastics. PLA biobased biodegradable plastics are made from renewable resources like corn starch and are compostable. PHA biobased biodegradable plastics are produced by bacteria and are fully biodegradable. Cellulose biobased biodegradable plastics are made from natural fibers and are also compostable. These types of biodegradable plastics offer a more sustainable alternative to conventional plastics.</p></p>
