<p><strong>The Ceramic Microactuators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cantilever-Type Ceramic Microactuator</li><li>Mobile Type Ceramic Microactuator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ceramic microactuators are categorized into two main types: Cantilever-Type and Mobile Type. Cantilever-Type microactuators operate by bending a thin strip of ceramic material, while Mobile Type microactuators use a mobile element to generate movement. Cantilever-Type microactuators are more suitable for applications requiring precise and delicate movements, such as in microscale sensors. On the other hand, Mobile Type microactuators are better suited for applications requiring larger displacements and forces, such as in microscale robotics.</p></p>
