<p><strong>The Sheet Flooring Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Heterogeneous Sheet Flooring</li><li>Homogeneous Sheet Flooring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sheet flooring comes in two main types: Heterogeneous Sheet Flooring and Homogeneous Sheet Flooring. Heterogeneous Sheet Flooring is made up of multiple layers, each serving a different purpose such as wear resistance, design, and stability. This type offers a wide variety of designs, colors, and finishes. On the other hand, Homogeneous Sheet Flooring is made of only one layer, providing a consistent color and pattern throughout. It is durable, easy to clean, and commonly used in high-traffic areas. Both types offer a range of benefits and cater to different preferences and requirements.</p></p>
