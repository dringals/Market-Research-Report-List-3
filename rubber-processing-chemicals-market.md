<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Rubber Processing Chemicals Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Antidegradants</li><li>Accelerators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rubber processing chemicals market includes antidegradants and accelerators. Antidegradants are additives that prevent rubber from breaking down due to environmental factors such as heat, light, and oxygen, extending the lifespan of rubber products. Accelerators, on the other hand, are chemicals that speed up the vulcanization process, allowing rubber to harden more quickly and retain its shape. Both antidegradants and accelerators play key roles in enhancing the performance and durability of rubber products.</p></p>
