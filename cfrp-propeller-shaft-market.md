<p><p>The CFRP propeller shaft market can be divided into two types: single piece and two piece propeller shafts. Single piece CFRP propeller shafts are made from a single solid piece of carbon fiber reinforced polymer, providing a more seamless and rigid structure. Two piece CFRP propeller shafts, on the other hand, are made from two separate pieces joined together, offering flexibility in design and installation. Both types of propeller shafts offer lightweight and durable solutions for high-performance vehicles.</p></p>
