<p><p>Convertible container ships are vessels that can carry both full container loads (FCL) and less than container loads (LCL). On the other hand, the LCL container ship market serves shippers with smaller loads that do not require a full container, providing flexibility and cost savings for those with less cargo volume. The convertible nature of these ships allows them to cater to a wide range of customer needs in the container shipping industry.</p></p>
