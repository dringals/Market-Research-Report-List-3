<p><p>Molecular sieve raw powders are classified into different types based on their composition and properties. A-Type molecular sieve raw powders are used for adsorbing water molecules and small polar molecules, making them suitable for applications such as drying gases and liquids. X-Type molecular sieve raw powders have a higher selectivity for adsorbing larger molecules, making them ideal for separating different components in a mixture. Both types of molecular sieve raw powders play a crucial role in various industries for purification, separation, and dehydration processes.</p></p>
