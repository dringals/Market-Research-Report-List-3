<p><p>Humic acid fertilizer can be classified into two main types: solid humic acid fertilizer and liquid humic acid fertilizer. Solid humic acid fertilizer is typically in granular form and is easier to store and handle. Both types of humic acid fertilizers provide essential nutrients to plants and improve soil structure, leading to increased crop yields and overall plant health.</p></p>
