<p><p>Breader premixes are used to coat food products before frying or baking. There are two main types of breader premixes: crumbs & flakes and flour & starch. Crumbs & flakes are made from ingredients like breadcrumbs, cornflakes, or cereal flakes, which add a crunchy texture to the food. Flour & starch-based breader premixes are made from a combination of flour and starch such as wheat flour, cornstarch, or rice flour, which provide a crispy coating to the food. These two types cater to different tastes and preferences in the market.</p></p>
