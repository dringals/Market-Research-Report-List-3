<p><p>Aluminum paste for printing ink market includes two main types: Leafing Aluminium Paste and Non-Leafing Aluminium Paste. Leafing Aluminium Paste consists of flaky particles that float to the surface, providing a shiny and reflective finish. On the other hand, Non-Leafing Aluminium Paste has finer particles that remain dispersed within the ink, offering a smoother and more subtle effect. Both types are used in various applications in the printing industry, depending on the desired outcome and appearance.</p></p>
