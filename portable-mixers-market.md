<p><ul><li>Direct Drive</li><li>Gear Drive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Portable mixers can be classified into two main types: direct drive and gear drive. Direct drive mixers have the motor directly attached to the mixing shaft, providing a more compact and simple design. On the other hand, gear drive mixers use gears to transfer power from the motor to the mixing shaft, allowing for higher torque and better control over the mixing process. Both types of mixers are commonly used in various industries such as food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, and chemicals for blending, mixing, and homogenizing purposes.</p></p>
