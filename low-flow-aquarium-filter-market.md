<p><p>The low flow aquarium filter market consists of three main types: mechanical filters, biological filters, and chemical filters. Mechanical filters work to physically remove solid waste from the tank, while biological filters promote the growth of beneficial bacteria to break down harmful substances. Chemical filters use activated carbon or ion exchange resins to remove impurities from the water. Each type of filter plays a crucial role in maintaining a clean and healthy aquatic environment for fish and other inhabitants.</p></p>
