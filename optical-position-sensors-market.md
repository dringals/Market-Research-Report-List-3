One Dimensional Optical Position Sensors measure linear movement along a single axis, while Two Dimensional Optical Position Sensors detect movement in two dimensions. Multi-Axial Optical Position Sensors are capable of measuring position along multiple axes simultaneously. These sensors are used in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and robotics for precise positioning and control applications. Each type of sensor offers different capabilities and advantages depending on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
