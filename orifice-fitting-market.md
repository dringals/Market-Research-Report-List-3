<p><ul><li>Dual Chamber Orifice Fitting</li><li>Single Chamber Orifice Fitting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Orifice fittings are commonly divided into two main types: Dual Chamber Orifice Fitting and Single Chamber Orifice Fitting. </p><p>Dual Chamber Orifice Fitting consists of two pressure chambers separated by the orifice plate, providing more accurate differential pressure measurements. </p><p>Single Chamber Orifice Fitting, on the other hand, has only one pressure chamber leading to simpler installation and maintenance. Each type caters to different industry needs and requirements, offering a range of options for measuring flow rates accurately.</p></p>
