<p><p>FBG temperature sensors are widely used in two main market types: distributed sensing and fiber optic point sensing. Distributed sensing involves embedding the sensors along a fiber optic cable to measure temperature variations continuously over a large area. Fiber optic point sensing, on the other hand, uses individual sensors placed at specific points to gather temperature data at those locations. Both market types offer accurate and reliable temperature monitoring solutions for various industries, including oil and gas, aerospace, and healthcare.</p></p>
