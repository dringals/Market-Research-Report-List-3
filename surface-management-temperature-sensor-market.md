<p><p>Surface management temperature sensor market consists of two main types: contact-type temperature sensors and noncontact-type temperature sensors.</p><p>Contact-type temperature sensors make direct physical contact with the surface they are measuring, providing accurate readings. Noncontact-type temperature sensors, on the other hand, measure temperature without physical contact, making them suitable for measuring moving or inaccessible surfaces.</p><p>Both types have their own advantages and applications, catering to different industries and requirements in managing surface temperature effectively.</p></p>
