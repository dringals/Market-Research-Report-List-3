<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Fans and Blowers</li><li>Axial Fans and Blowers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial fans and blowers market are segmented into two main types: centrifugal fans and blowers, and axial fans and blowers. Centrifugal fans and blowers use rotating impellers to increase the speed and pressure of the air, making them ideal for applications requiring higher air pressure. Axial fans and blowers, on the other hand, use blades that force air to move parallel to the shaft, making them suitable for applications requiring a high volume of air flow at a lower pressure.</p></p>
