<p><p>Fiberglass wall coverings come in two main types: non-woven fabric and woven fabric. Non-woven fabric is made by bonding fibers together using heat and chemicals, creating a strong and durable material. Woven fabric, on the other hand, is made by weaving fibers together, resulting in a more flexible and breathable material. Both types of fiberglass wall coverings provide protection and insulation for walls, making them popular choices in the construction and interior design industries.</p></p>
