<p><ul><li>Spring Air Rifle</li><li>Gas Piston Air Rifle</li><li>Precharged Pneumatic Air Rifle</li><li>CO2 Powered Air Rifle</li><li>Variable Pump Air Rifle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Air rifles come in various types to meet different shooting needs. Spring air rifles are powered by a spring and require cocking before each shot. Gas piston air rifles use a gas-filled cylinder instead of a spring for smoother shooting. Precharged pneumatic air rifles have a built-in air reservoir that needs to be filled with a separate pump. CO2 powered air rifles use disposable CO2 cartridges for power. Variable pump air rifles allow the shooter to control the power by pumping a lever multiple times.</p></p>
