<p><p>Hands-free devices market is divided into two types - wireless and wired. Wireless hands-free devices use Bluetooth technology to connect to a smartphone or other device, allowing for greater freedom of movement. They are popular for use in cars, while exercising, or at work. Wired hands-free devices, on the other hand, physically connect to the device through a cable. They are often more affordable and reliable than wireless options, but may limit the user's range of movement.</p></p>
