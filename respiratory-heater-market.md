<p><p>The respiratory heater market includes two main types: benchtop respiratory heaters and portable respiratory heaters. Benchtop respiratory heaters are designed to be stationary and are typically used in a clinical or laboratory setting. They provide a controlled heat source for respiratory therapy treatments. Portable respiratory heaters, on the other hand, are lightweight and compact devices that can be easily transported and used in various environments, such as at home or while traveling. Both types of respiratory heaters are essential tools in providing effective heating therapy for respiratory conditions.</p></p>
