<p><p>High pressure pipe fittings market can be divided into various types such as straight fitting, elbow fitting, three-way fitting, four-way fitting, and others. Straight fittings are used to connect two straight pipes, while elbow fittings are used for changing the direction of flow. Three-way fittings are used to connect three pipes at different angles, and four-way fittings are used to connect four pipes. Other fittings include couplings, adapters, and plugs that serve various purposes in high-pressure piping systems.</p></p>
