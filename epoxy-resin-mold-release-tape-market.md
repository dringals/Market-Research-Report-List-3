<p><strong>The Epoxy Resin Mold Release Tape Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Side</li><li>Double Side</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Epoxy resin mold release tape is available in two main types: single side and double side. Single side release tape has adhesive on one side, which allows for easy application onto surfaces when preparing molds. On the other hand, double side release tape has adhesive on both sides, providing a secure bond between the mold and the tape. Both types offer effective release properties, preventing the epoxy resin from sticking to the mold and facilitating easy removal after curing.</p></p>
