<p><p>The scratch remover market can be divided into two main types: solid type and emulsion type. Solid type scratch removers typically come in the form of wax or paste that needs to be rubbed onto the scratched surface to fill in the scratch and restore the finish. On the other hand, emulsion type scratch removers are liquid-based products that can be applied directly onto the scratch and often contain additional ingredients to help polish and blend the scratched area with the surrounding surface.</p></p>
