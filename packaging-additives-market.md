<p><p>Packaging additives play a crucial role in enhancing the functionality and shelf life of packaging materials. UV stabilizers protect packaging materials from degradation caused by exposure to sunlight, increasing their lifespan. Anti-fog agents prevent condensation on packaging surfaces, maintaining visibility of the products inside. Clarifying agents improve the optical clarity of packaging materials, while antistatic agents reduce static electricity buildup, ensuring easier handling and transport.</p></p>
