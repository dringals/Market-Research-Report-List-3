<p><strong>The Polished Round-Grained Rice Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Translucent Rice</li><li>Opaque Rice</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polished Round-Grained Rice is categorized into two types in the market - Translucent Rice and Opaque Rice. Translucent Rice has a clear and shiny appearance due to a higher starch content, making it sticky when cooked. It is commonly used in sushi and sticky rice dishes. Opaque Rice, on the other hand, has a dull appearance and a lower starch content, resulting in separate grains when cooked. It is ideal for dishes where fluffy and separate grains are preferred, such as biryani or pilaf.</p></p>
