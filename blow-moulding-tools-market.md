<p><ul><li>Extrusion</li><li>Injection</li><li>Stretch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Blow moulding tools are categorized into three main types based on the manufacturing process they employ: Extrusion, Injection, and Stretch. </p><p>Extrusion blow moulding tools use a hollow tube (parison) that is heated and inflated to the desired shape inside a mould. Injection blow moulding tools inject molten material into a mould cavity, forming the desired shape. Stretch blow moulding tools involve stretching preforms in both axial and radial directions before blowing air into them to create the final product. Each type of blow moulding tool offers unique advantages and is used in specific applications based on the manufacturing requirements.</p></p>
