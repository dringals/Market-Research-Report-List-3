<p><p>Nonwoven Cotton Market includes various types such as Cotton Spunlace and Needle-punched Cotton. Cotton Spunlace involves a process where the fibers are entangled through high-pressure water jets, resulting in a soft and smooth texture. On the other hand, Needle-punched Cotton is created by mechanically punching the fibers together with barbed needles, producing a durable and resilient material. Both market types are popular for their versatility and applications in industries such as hygiene products, apparel, and construction.</p></p>
