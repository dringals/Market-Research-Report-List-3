<p><p>Synthetic geotextile market can be categorized into four major types based on the material used: Polypropylene, Polyester (PET), Polyethylene, and Others. Polypropylene geotextiles are durable, resistant to UV degradation, and commonly used in erosion control applications. Polyester geotextiles, made from PET, are known for their high tensile strength and are commonly used in road construction and drainage systems. Polyethylene geotextiles are flexible and commonly used in landfill construction. The Others category includes geotextiles made from materials such as nylon and aramid.</p></p>
