<p><p>RAID technology is utilized in two main market types: Hardware RAID Cards and Software RAID Cards. Hardware RAID Cards are physical cards that are installed into a computer system and provide dedicated processing power for managing RAID configurations. Software RAID Cards, on the other hand, are software-based solutions that utilize the computer's existing processing power to manage RAID configurations. Both options offer benefits and drawbacks, such as increased reliability and performance with Hardware RAID Cards, and cost-effectiveness and flexibility with Software RAID Cards.</p></p>
