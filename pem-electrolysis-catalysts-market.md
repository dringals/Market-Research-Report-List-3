<p><p>PEM Electrolysis Catalysts are used in Proton Exchange Membrane (PEM) electrolyzers to enable the efficient production of hydrogen and oxygen from water through electrolysis. The market for PEM electrolysis catalysts can be categorized into two main types: Oxygen Evolution Reaction (OER) Catalysts and Hydrogen Evolution Reaction (HER) Catalysts. OER catalysts are responsible for the generation of oxygen gas during the electrolysis process, while HER catalysts facilitate the production of hydrogen gas. Both types of catalysts play a crucial role in advancing the development of clean hydrogen technology.</p></p>
