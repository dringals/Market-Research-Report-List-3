<p><p>NDT equipment market includes various types such as Magnetic Particle Testing, Visual Testing, Liquid Penetrant Testing, Radiographic Testing, Ultrasonic Testing, and others. Magnetic Particle Testing is used to detect surface and near-surface defects. Visual Testing involves the use of visual aids to detect flaws. Liquid Penetrant Testing is used to detect surface-breaking defects. Radiographic Testing uses X-rays to detect internal flaws. Ultrasonic Testing uses sound waves to detect flaws inside materials. Other methods include eddy current testing, remote visual inspection, and acoustic emission testing.</p></p>
